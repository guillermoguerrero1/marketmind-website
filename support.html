Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with MarketMind?

Getting started with MarketMind is easy:

  1. Download the app from the App Store or Google Play
  2. Create your account or sign in
  3. Start logging your first trade
  4. Explore the analytics and features
How do I log a new trade?

To log a new trade:

  1. Tap the "+" button on the main screen
  2. Enter the symbol (stock, crypto, forex, or futures)
  3. Fill in entry and exit details
  4. Add notes and emotional state
  5. Save your trade
Can I sync my data across devices?

Yes! MarketMind offers optional cloud synchronization. Enable this feature in your settings to:

  • Access your trading journal from any device
  • Backup your data automatically
  • Restore your account if needed
How do I analyze my trading performance?

MarketMind provides comprehensive performance analytics:

  • View your win/loss ratio
  • Analyze profit/loss trends
  • Track emotional patterns
  • Identify your best performing strategies
Is my trading data secure?

Absolutely! We take data security seriously:

  • Your data is primarily stored locally on your device
  • Cloud sync uses industry-standard encryption
  • We never share your personal trading data
  • You have full control over your data
